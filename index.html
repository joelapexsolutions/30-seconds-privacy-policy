Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how The Ultimate 30 Seconds Game handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Player and Team Names: Names you enter for players and teams during gameplay
  • Game Settings: Your preferences for sound, power-ups, categories, and game configuration
  • Custom Content: Any custom questions or categories you create

Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Statistics: Number of games played, question history (to avoid repeats)
  • Technical Data: App crashes or errors (no personal information included)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and improve the game experience
  • Save your game preferences and custom content
  • Enable multiplayer functionality
  • Remember your team setups for replay
  • Prevent question repetition during gameplay

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Game settings and preferences
  • Custom questions and categories
  • Team setups and player names
  • Game statistics

Network Play

When using network multiplayer features:

  • Game session data is temporarily stored on secure servers
  • Data is automatically deleted when the game session ends
  • No personal information is permanently stored on our servers

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your information may only be shared in these limited circumstances:

  • With other players during network games (only team/player names)
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • In the event of a business transfer (with the same privacy protections)

5. Children's Privacy

The Ultimate 30 Seconds Game is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View what data we have about you
  • Delete: Remove your data from our systems
  • Correct: Update any incorrect information
  • Control: Manage your privacy settings within the app

7. Third-Party Services

The Ultimate 30 Seconds Game uses these third-party services:

8. International Users

The Ultimate 30 Seconds Game is available worldwide. Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, but we ensure the same level of protection regardless of location.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date below
  • Posting a notice in the app
  • Sending a notification through app stores (for major changes)
